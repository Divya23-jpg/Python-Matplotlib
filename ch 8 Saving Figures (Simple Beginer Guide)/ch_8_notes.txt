
Why To Save Charts:

1.For documentation and reports (academic, project, presentations).
2.To ensure reusability without regenerating plots.
3.For sharing and collaboration with others.
4.To maintain publication-quality visuals (high resolution, vector formats).
5.For automation in workflows (dashboards, CI/CD, scheduled reports).
6.To preserve analysis results for future reference.
7.To embed charts in websites or applications
