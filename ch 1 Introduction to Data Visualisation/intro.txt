

What is Data Visualization?

1.Data Visualization is the scientific and technical process of representing data in graphical or visual formats—
2.such as charts, graphs, maps, and dashboards
3.to communicate information clearly, efficiently, and effectively.
4.transform the raw data into visuals



Application of Data Visualization
1.Data science: Simplyfying data to explain in data science
2.Business/Marketing: Identify sales, trends and customer behaviour in visual formats—
3.Healthcare: Moniterising health metrices and disease spread in Healthcare
4.Finance: Analyzing stock price and Financial performance
5.Education: Tracking student performance over time in Education.
6.Government/Policy: Presenting public data like census and population level



Python Libraries for Visualization:
1.Matplotlib:Basic and powerful ploting
2.Seaborn: Statistical plots build on Matplotlib
3.Plotify: Intrective,web based Visualization 
4.Pandas(plot): Quick plots from DataFrames



Advantages of Data Visualization:
1.Saves Time: Understand Data quikly
2.Easy communication: Explain data to anyone
3.Spot Trends and Patterns: See Data's Movement
4.Better Decision Making: Make informed choices
5.Handles Big Data Well: Understand thousands of rows
